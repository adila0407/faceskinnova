User Manual

Welcome to Face Skin Nova!

This user manual is designed to guide you through using the features of our website. Whether you're a beginner or a skincare expert, this guide will help you get the most out of our platform.

1. How to Navigate the Website

Our website is organized into several sections:

  • Home: Explore our skincare products, latest offerings, and promotions.
  • Products: View detailed product information, including ingredients, prices, and how-to-use guides.
  • Reviews: Read customer reviews and share your own experiences with the products.
  • User Manual: Find this guide and other helpful information about using the site.

2. How to Use Products

Here are the steps for using our skincare products:

  • Cleanser: Apply the cleanser to a damp face, gently massage in circular motions, then rinse off with warm water. Use morning and night.
  • Moisturizer: After cleansing, apply a generous amount of moisturizer to your face and neck. Use daily.
  • Serum: Apply a few drops of serum to your face after moisturizing, focusing on areas with more visible concerns. Use daily.
  • Sunscreen: Always apply sunscreen in the morning to protect your skin from harmful UV rays.

3. How to Leave a Review

To leave a review for a product:

  • Go to the Reviews section.
  • Fill out the form with your name and review text.
  • Click Submit Review to share your feedback with others.
